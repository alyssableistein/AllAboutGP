  • If you adopt a pair of guinea pigs then it's likely they will already have an established bond.

  • Guinea pigs are great pets for children and live long lives compared to hamsters.

Things to Consider Before Adopting

  • Guinea pigs do best with a companion.
  • Guinea pigs require a substantial commitment.
  • Guinea pigs require vegetables and hay, in addition to guinea pig food.
  • Guinea pigs require daily vitamin C supplementations.
  • Guinea pigs require a large enclosures.
  • Guinea pigs can be noisy.
